3SHIPPING INFORMATION

All packages ship from our Khmelnitskyi, Ukraine. Domestic orders placed by 12:00 pm PT ship the same day, excluding backordered items, weekends and shipping holidays. Orders placed on a weekend or shipping holiday will ship the next business day. Shipping estimates begin the day after your order has shipped and also exclude weekends and shipping holidays. For example, a Two Day Air order shipped on Thursday will be delivered the following Monday.
